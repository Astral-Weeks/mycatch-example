Why MyCatch?


The more information we have about our fish populations, the better we can sustain our waterways and understand their current health and status, which consequently helps all of our anglers.

The people who know our fish best are our anglers. By sharing as much information as you can, or wish to, about the number of fish you catch, release or take home, and what the size or weight of the catch is, you can help us better estimate the current population numbers in our rivers.
